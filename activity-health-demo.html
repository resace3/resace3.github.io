What is this estimate?

A transparent teaching simulation combines age, BMI, selected fictional health history, and virtual step volume. It is not the research model and cannot estimate a real person's health.

How is the rhythm built?

Each selectable virtual profile has a synthetic 24-hour curve. Generating a new week adds deterministic variation so the demo remains reproducible.
